Ghost Roulette

Rules:

  • There are 16 cards total
  • There is 1 ghost card. If you flip over the ghost card, you lose 4 points and the round ends.
  • There are 7 special cards.
  • During a turn, player flips over a card and if its not the ghost card, the game continues. You must flip atleast 1 card every turn. After that, you can either end your turn, or go in for more.
